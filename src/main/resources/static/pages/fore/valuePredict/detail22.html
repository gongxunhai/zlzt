由化学交联法固载的固-固相变材料的制备方法实质审查中国发明

Preparation of solid solid phase change material immobilized by chemical crosslinking method

摘要

一种由化学交联法固载的固-固相变材料的制备方法，将丙烯酸聚乙二醇酯溶于去离子水中，加入交联剂和引发剂，将体系温度升至70～80℃，反应2～5h，形成具有交联网络结构的水凝胶，将水凝胶于35℃干燥24h，即得到固-固相变材料丙烯酸聚乙二醇酯凝胶；其中，所述交联剂与丙烯酸聚乙二醇酯的质量比为1∶200～7∶20，引发剂与丙烯酸聚乙二醇酯的质量比为1∶200～1∶50，去离子水与丙烯酸聚乙二醇酯的体积比为6∶1～10∶1。本发明通过化学反应改变丙烯酸聚乙二醇酯原有的化学结构，实现不同温度下的固-固相转变，并通过对反应条件的控制，改变产品的交联程度，从而调节相变温度和相变焓，以满足不同的实际要求。


[ 翻译 Translate ]

A preparation method of solid-solid phase change material immobilized by chemical crosslinking method is described. Polyethylene glycol acrylate is dissolved in deionized water, crosslinking agent and initiator are added, the temperature of the system is raised to 70-80 C, reaction time is 2-5 h, and the hydrogel with crosslinking structure is formed. The solid-solid phase change is obtained by drying the hydrogel at 35 C for 24 h. The mass ratio of the crosslinking agent to polyethylene glycol acrylate is 1: 200~7: 20, the mass ratio of initiator to acrylic polyethylene glycol is 1: 200~1: 50, and the volume ratio of deionized water to acrylic polyethylene glycol is 6: 1~10: 1. The invention changes the original chemical structure of polyethylene glycol acrylate by chemical reaction, realizes solid-solid phase transition at different temperatures, and changes the degree of cross-linking of the product by controlling the reaction conditions, thereby adjusting the phase change temperature and the phase change enthalpy to meet different practical requirements.
